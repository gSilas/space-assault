
List of Releasekeys:
	
	WASD - movement of ship
	LMouse - Shoot laser
	RMouse - Shoot photonbomb
	B - opens shop (if near station and with sufficient funds)


List of Debuggingkeys:

	N - activates mouse click spawning of enemies (Boids.cs)
	K+L - adds 10000 to your money (GameplayScreen.cs)
	F+G - deals 1000 dmg(health) to the station (GameplayScreen.cs)
	F+H - deals 1000 dmg(shield) to the station (GameplayScreen.cs)
	L+H - adds wave

	Console Output:
    F1 - Drone Damage
    F2 - Drone Shield
    F3 - Drone Health
