=========================
- Deus Ex console fonts -
=========================

Date:  July 20th, 2006
Version 1.0
ZIP contents:  DXconsoleBold.tff, DXconsoleRegular.ttf, this readme document.
Author:  Sivak Drac
Project time:  1 week

Description:  These are the fonts seen in the computer game Deus Ex for the console and most menus.  The bold font 
is typically on buttons whereas the regular is for most text on e-mails, dialogue, etc.  I did these up in TTF form 
as I liked the fonts and thought it'd be an interesting project.
